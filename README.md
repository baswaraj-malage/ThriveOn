# Privacy Policy for ThriveOn

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

## Introduction

ThriveOn ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of young athletes and their guardians. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Mobile Number**: Used for authentication and account access
- **Profile Information**: Name, age, gender, sport, height, weight, weekly training hours
- **Health Data**: Daily meal entries, water intake, sleep hours, body weight tracking

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features accessed, session duration
- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Authenticate users and maintain secure access to the app
- Provide personalized diet plans based on sport and profile
- Track nutrition and health habits over time
- Display motivational content and nutrition tips
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- **All user data is stored locally** on your device using encrypted storage
- No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services
- Data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties

### Security Measures
- Session tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- OTP authentication provides secure access
- Data is protected by device-level security (PIN, biometric authentication)

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
- You can delete individual entries or your entire profile at any time
- Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device

## Children's Privacy

ThriveOn is designed for young athletes (ages 10-18). We comply with applicable children's privacy laws:
- We collect only essential information needed for app functionality
- No data is shared with third parties
- Parents/guardians should supervise app usage for children under 13
- We do not use data for advertising or marketing purposes

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information stored in the app
- Update or correct your profile information
- Delete your entries or entire profile
- Export your data (future feature)

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your personal information
- Use your data for advertising
- Transmit data to external servers (all data stays on your device)

## Third-Party Services

ThriveOn does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline with local data storage.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through:
- In-app notifications
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
